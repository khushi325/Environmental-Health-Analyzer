🌍 Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

A real-time air & water-quality intelligence dashboard. We pulled the latest available AQI readings for 17 Indian cities — including Hāpur, your detected location — cleaned and cross-checked them, and turned them into a personal environmental health report.

📍 Default city: Hāpur, Uttar Pradesh
🗓️ Data captured: 28–29 Jun 2026
🧪 Quality check: 17/17 cities validated
📡 Scale: CPCB AQI bands
🔍 Data & methodology note: AQI, PM2.5 and PM10 readings are sourced from live public monitors (aqicn.org World Air Quality Index project, aqi.in, IQAir) captured 28–29 Jun 2026. Where a station reported AQI but not separate PM2.5/PM10 sub-indices, those two values were estimated from the AQI using standard CPCB conversion ratios and flagged "est." on the city card. Water-quality scores are a composite illustrative index built from publicly documented regional groundwater-stress and river-pollution patterns (CGWB / CPCB reporting trends) since no single live per-city water-quality API exists — treat these as directional, not laboratory-grade. All records passed range validation (0–500 AQI) and duplicate/missing-value checks before analysis.
